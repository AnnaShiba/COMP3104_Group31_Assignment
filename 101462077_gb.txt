Information about George Brown College

George Brown is a Canadian College located in Toronto, Ontario. 
To learn more about George Brown College, check out these web pages:
https://www.georgebrown.ca/
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Brown_College 

George Brown College, specifically the Casa Loma Campus, is home to the School of Computer Technology and 
the Computer Programming and Analysic Program. There are two streams of this program, regular (T177) and 
online (T197). I, Nicole, am part of the online program.
To learn more about T197, check out this web page:
